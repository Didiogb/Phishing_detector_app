About Our Phishing Detector

The AI Phishing Website Detector is built to protect users from malicious websites disguised as trusted platforms. Using advanced machine learning (XGBoost) and intelligent URL analysis, it identifies suspicious domains in real time. By constantly learning from live datasets of legitimate and phishing websites, the system ensures reliable, up-to-date threat detection. SecureScan empowers safe online browsing through AI-driven security awareness and automation.
